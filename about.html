    Pioneering the Future of Engineering

    The School of Engineering & Technology (SOET) is a premier University Teaching Department of Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya, Ujjain. Established in 2011, we have grown into a dynamic hub of technological education and research.

    Sanctioned by the AICTE and the Department of Technical Education (MP), SOET offers robust undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech) programs. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical industry demands, ensuring our graduates emerge as innovators, problem-solvers, and leaders.

    Our Vision

    To be a globally recognized center of excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation, producing technically sound and socially responsible professionals who can lead the technological advancement of society.

    Our Mission

    • To impart high-quality technical education through modern pedagogical methods.
    • To foster a culture of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
    • To establish strong industry-academia collaborations.
    • To inculcate strong ethical values and leadership qualities.

    Our Legacy & History

    2011

    Establishment of the School of Engineering and Technology under the faculty of Engineering.

    2015

    First batch of B.Tech students successfully graduates, with 70% securing campus placements.

    2018

    Introduction of Postgraduate (M.Tech) programs in Structural and Thermal Engineering.

    2023

    SOET awarded 'A+' grade in recent NAAC accreditation cycle and inauguration of the new AI Lab.
